FAQ

Common questions

What is biomechanics analysis? Biomechanics analysis is the study of the mechanical aspects of human movement, focusing on factors such as force, motion, and energy involved in athletic performance.
How can biomechanics analysis benefit athletes? Biomechanics analysis can help athletes improve their technique, prevent injuries, and optimize their performance by providing insights into their movement patterns and mechanics.
Who can benefit from biomechanics analysis services? Athletes of all levels, from beginners to professionals, can benefit from biomechanics analysis services to enhance their training, performance, and overall athletic development.
What is the process of biomechanics analysis like? The process of biomechanics analysis typically involves capturing movement data (video) through various technologies such as motion capture systems, analyzing the data to identify key biomechanical parameters, and providing feedback and recommendations for improvement.
