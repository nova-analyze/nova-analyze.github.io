Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to analyze our video?

A: Generally, all analysis (block start + max velocity) will be done in a few hours during working time.



Q: Any Discount?  

A: We will post promo code in our YouTube & Instagram video comments section from time to time. Secondly, anyone purchased 3+ times analysis in one run will get up to 60% off discount.

Every day from 10AM to 1PM,

Q: Is my transactions/payment safe?

A: Every payment will be processed on Stripe, we don't hold the transaction platform and we don't hold your bank information.



Q: What data source do you use?

A: Data mainly obtained from Dr. Ralph Mann’s book, The Mechanics of Sprinting and Hurdling



Q: After analysis, what else can we get?

A: We will provide you a general training idea and structure to improve your weakness, it may contain videos (exercise examples). You can also send videos to let us check whether your forms (running form/gym exercises) are correct.



Q: Do you only analyze sprinters mechanics?

A: We will develop models for hurdlers, long jumpers, and probably shot put athletes (glide technique). All these sports will be updated soon.



Q: What service do you recommend to purchase?

A: Beginner athletes we recommend you to purchase the General Sprint Training Plan only.

If your average 100m time is between (Men 10.1 to 12.00s) (Women 10.9s to 13.00s), we highly recommend you to purchase Biomechanics Analysis.

If you want to see a huge progress in a short period of time (4 months), and you can make sure that you will keep your training consistency, we recommend you to purchase 1 on 1 training.



Q: Is there a refund policy?

A: Yes, you can request the refund before the completion of analysis task, for example, if you think the analysis process takes too long time (more than 24 hours), you can request the refund immediately through the Stripe platform, once the analysis is done and has been sent to you, no refund.
